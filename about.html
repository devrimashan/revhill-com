ABOUT THE COMPANY

RevHill Global
Holdings LLC

We build mobile applications that help professionals in Turkey and beyond prepare for government-administered certification exams. Our products are available on the Apple App Store and Google Play.

What We Do

RevHill develops exam preparation software for vocational and professional certification programs regulated by the Turkish Ministry of National Education (MEB — Milli Eğitim Bakanlığı).

Our flagship product, Manikürist Sınav Hazırlık, is a mobile app that provides practice exams, flashcards, and performance analytics for candidates preparing for the MEB Manicurist certification exam.

Our Approach

We prioritize user privacy: our apps are fully offline-first, storing all data locally on the user's device. We do not collect personal information, require account registration, or use advertising networks.

In-app purchases are handled securely through Apple App Store and Google Play using RevenueCat, a trusted payment infrastructure provider.
