Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

RevHill Global Holdings LLC ("RevHill", "we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used when you use the Manikürist Sınav Hazırlık application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device (Never Transmitted)

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's on-device storage. It is never sent to our servers or any third party:

  • Exam history and results
  • Wrong and blank answers (Hatalarım feature)
  • Performance statistics (exams taken, best score, pass count)
  • Premium membership status

Purchase Data (RevenueCat)

We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases through the Apple App Store. RevenueCat processes only your purchase history and subscription status to verify your entitlement. No personal information beyond what Apple provides for the transaction is shared. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Data transmitted to RevenueCat: purchase receipt, anonymous device identifier (provided by Apple).

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

Our App does not:

  • Require account registration or login
  • Collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Access your location
  • Use your camera or microphone
  • Use third-party advertising networks
  • Send usage analytics to any server
  • Track you across other apps or websites

4. Apple Privacy Nutrition Label

Data Linked to You:

  • Purchase History — Used for providing purchased content (RevenueCat/Apple IAP)

Data Not Linked to You:

  • App Functionality — Exam history, scores, wrong answers (stored on-device only)

Data Not Collected:

  • Contact Info, Health, Financial, Location, Browsing, Search, Identifiers, Diagnostics, Usage Data

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored app data at any time using the "Reset Data" option within the app settings. Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data from your device. Since we do not maintain user accounts or server-side data, there is no account deletion process required.
